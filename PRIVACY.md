## Privacy & Usage Notice

This tool processes files locally on your computer to identify duplicated files.<br>
- All data is processed **entirely in memory and on-device**.
- No data is uploaded, transmitted, or shared over the internet.
- The application may generate local error and diagnostic logs to help with debugging and stability.
- These logs **do not contain** images, videos, text, files, personal or device information.

All processing happens locally using open-source libraries.<br>
Any data generated by this tool remains **entirely under your control**.
